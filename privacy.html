FloraChain ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users — Farmers, Agents, Store Owners, Vehicle Owners, and Drivers — who use our floral supply chain marketplace platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. நாங்கள் சேகரிக்கும் தகவல்கள் — Information We Collect

When you create an account or use FloraChain, we collect the following:

  • Identity Information: First name, last name, gender — used for account personalization.
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, email address — used for OTP verification, notifications and account recovery.
  • Address Information: Full address, pincode, state — required for delivery logistics and regional market intelligence.
  • KYC Documents: Aadhaar number, front and back images of Aadhaar card. For Drivers: Driving License number and image. These documents are collected solely for identity verification and fraud prevention.
  • Business Preferences: Flower types you buy or sell, billing frequency setting — shared voluntarily to enable marketplace matching.
  • Financial Data: Transaction history, payment amounts, UPI IDs (user-provided) — stored in your private ledger visible only to you.
  • Location Data: For Drivers and Vehicle Owners — real-time GPS coordinates are collected during active deliveries to power our live tracking feature.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, clicks — collected automatically to improve platform performance.

2. தகவல்களின் பயன்பாடு — How We Use Your Information

  • Identity Verification: To prevent fake registrations and ensure only genuine users transact on the platform.
  • Marketplace Matching: Your flower preferences (what you grow/buy/sell) are visible to other verified users so they can discover and contact you.
  • Financial Tracking: Your income, expenses, and payment history are stored in your personal ledger — accessible only by you.
  • Live Tracking: Driver location data is shared only with the specific buyer and seller of an active order.
  • Notifications: SMS and email alerts for new orders, payment confirmations, price changes, and billing reminders.
  • Platform Improvement: Anonymized usage data helps us improve the product experience.

3. தகவல்களைப் பகிர்தல் — Information Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We share minimal information only in these cases:

  • Between Trade Parties: When a buyer places an order, their delivery address is shared with the assigned driver. The seller's pickup location is also shared. This is essential for delivery.
  • Marketplace Discovery: Your business name, flower types, and billing cycle preference are visible on your public profile to other verified FloraChain users.
  • Payment Processors: If you use integrated payment gateways (UPI, bank transfer), your payment details are processed by RBI-compliant third parties. We do not store your full banking credentials.
  • Legal Requirement: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: In case of a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred with adequate privacy protections in place.

4. தரவு பாதுகாப்பு — Data Security

  • All data is stored on encrypted, secure servers in India.
  • KYC documents (Aadhaar images) are stored with AES-256 encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text.
  • All communication between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Your financial ledger is private — only you can see your own income and expenses.
  • We undergo regular security audits and penetration testing.

5. உங்கள் உரிமைகள் — Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit Preferences: Update your flower types, billing frequency, rates, and profile information anytime from your dashboard.
  • Data Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you by contacting support.
  • Data Deletion: Request account deletion. Your data will be removed within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-out of Notifications: Manage SMS/email notification preferences in your account settings.
  • Driver Availability: Vehicle Owners and Drivers can toggle their real-time availability status at any time — disabling location sharing during off-duty periods.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in across sessions.
  • Remember your preferences (language, dashboard settings).
  • Analyze platform usage with anonymized analytics.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.
